**Top News**

Japan is recasting its postwar defense doctrine as regional tensions escalate, with Prime Minister Takaichi pushing constitutional revisions while [joining Trump's](https://www.reuters.com/business/aerospace-defense/japan-join-trumps-golden-dome-project-expects-missile-requests-2026-03-13/) missile defense "Golden Dome" project. Bloomberg reports the [defense overhaul](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/features/2026-03-13/japan-s-sanae-takaichi-pushes-to-revise-pacifist-constitution) comes as East Asia sits on edge following Iran's military escalation, which has sent oil prices soaring and prompted Tokyo to [release reserves](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-13/japan-to-sell-oil-from-national-reserves-at-pre-iran-war-prices) at pre-war pricing.

The ruling coalition [rammed through](https://english.kyodonews.net/articles/-/72259) the fiscal 2026 budget in the lower house as Takaichi battles cold symptoms ahead of her crucial Trump meeting. The Yomiuri reports Japan's Defense Agency will [develop an](https://japannews.yomiuri.co.jp/politics/defense-security/20260314-316288/) AI intelligence analysis system for ground forces by 2027, while the government establishes a [nuclear power](https://japannews.yomiuri.co.jp/politics/politics-government/20260313-316297/) personnel command center to reverse post-Fukushima industry decline.

**Business & Markets**

• JR East [raised fares](http://www3.nhk.or.jp/news/html/20260314/k10015075381000.html) today for the first time since the company's founding, excluding consumption tax adjustments, according to NHK.

• Finance Minister Katayama warned Japan is [ready to](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/japan-ready-act-against-sharp-yen-swings-finance-minister-says-2026-03-13/) act against sharp yen swings, though Reuters notes the [intervention bar](https://www.reuters.com/sustainability/boards-policy-regulation/why-japans-bar-yen-intervention-is-now-higher-2026-03-13/) is now higher following policy shifts.

• Japan will [consider buying](https://www.reuters.com/sustainability/boards-policy-regulation/japan-consider-whether-buy-russian-crude-following-us-sanctions-waiver-2026-03-13/) Russian crude following the US sanctions waiver, Reuters reports, as global oil markets remain volatile.

• Asian nations are set to [pledge $30](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-13/asia-set-to-pledge-30-billion-in-energy-mineral-deals-with-us) billion in energy and mineral deals with the US, per Bloomberg, as Trump seeks to reduce China dependency.

**Government & Policy**
