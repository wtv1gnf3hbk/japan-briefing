Good morning from Tokyo. Japan marks [15 years](http://www3.nhk.or.jp/news/html/20260311/k10015071711000.html) since the Great East Japan Earthquake today, with ongoing challenges in Fukushima highlighting the long road to recovery. [NHK reports](http://www3.nhk.or.jp/news/html/20260311/k10015071781000.html) that TEPCO's target of completing Fukushima Daiichi's decommissioning by 2051 remains without a clear timeline, while [seismic activity](http://www3.nhk.or.jp/news/html/20260311/k10015072151000.html) in the earthquake zone has decreased but continues at active levels. The disaster's legacy extends beyond infrastructure — affected communities still struggle with [ongoing support](http://www3.nhk.or.jp/news/html/20260311/k10015071711000.html) for displaced residents and economic revival in coastal areas like [Ofunato](https://www.japantimes.co.jp/news/2026/03/11/japan/society/311-anniversary-ofunato/).

Meanwhile, the war between the US and Iran is creating immediate economic pressures for Japanese industry. [Bloomberg reports](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-10/iran-war-forces-japanese-auto-parts-suppliers-to-turn-to-rusal) that auto-parts suppliers are scrambling to secure aluminum supplies from Russian producer Rusal as Middle East tensions disrupt global commodity flows. Prime Minister Takaichi announced via Twitter that a second government charter flight carrying Japanese nationals departed Riyadh yesterday, bringing evacuees from Kuwait, Bahrain, Qatar and Saudi Arabia.

**Business & Markets**

• Japan [confirmed](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-09/japan-confirms-economy-expanded-as-takaichi-urges-investment) its economy expanded in Q4 as business investment surged, according to Bloomberg, with revised GDP data showing stronger growth than initially reported

• The government unveiled investment targets including [AI, quantum computing](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-10/japan-s-investment-targets-include-ai-quantum-computing-drones) and drones as part of its growth strategy, with [Reuters reporting](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/japan-targets-fivefold-rise-domestically-made-chip-sales-by-2040-2026-03-10/) Japan aims for a fivefold increase in domestic chip sales by 2040

• Japanese stocks [rebounded](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-10/japanese-stocks-rebound-as-oil-s-pullback-fuels-dip-buying) as oil prices pulled back from war-driven highs, according to Bloomberg, while [Bain Capital raised](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-10/bain-capital-raises-12-5-billion-in-asia-japan-fund-sprint) $12.5 billion across Asia and Japan funds

• Australia's Lynas [revised](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/australias-lynas-revamps-deal-supply-rare-earths-japan-2026-03-10/) its rare earth supply agreement with Japan, Reuters reports, while Japan's energy minister [backed](https://english.kyodonews.net/articles/-/72022) coordinated oil reserve releases to stabilize markets

**Government & Policy**

• [Reuters reports](https://www.reuters.com/world/asia-pacific/japans-takaichi-faces-fresh-scrutiny-over-boj-policy-stance-2026-03-10/) PM Takaichi faces renewed questions over her Bank of Japan policy stance as markets watch for signals on monetary easing
